Chenglong Shao
Research Professor
Network and Security Research Lab (NetLab), Korea University
Email: chenglong@korea.ac.kr
I am a Research Professor at Korea University. I received my Ph.D. degree in 2019 at Korea University, focusing on the development of advanced PHY and MAC algorithms in wireless and mobile systems, under the supervision of Prof. Wonjun Lee. Before joining NetLab, I learned Korean for about two years at Korea University. I received my B.S. degree in 2010 at Xi'an Jiaotong University.
